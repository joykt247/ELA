Welcome to ELA 7 at OHVA with Ms. T!

This site is for YOU!

  • The purpose of this site is to help you get acclimated to the online learning environment at OHVA (Ohio Virtual Academy) This is your school. Go Cardinals!.

How to Use this Website

  • I offer tips and tricks to help you navigate through the OLS (Online School) This is the platform students need to log into everyday to see their schedule, join Class Connect links, and access their class Content, Progress, and Grades., specific courses, and CCs (Class Connect) This is the live content class that students should be attending for the full 45 minutes Monday through Thursday.

  • Accessing Your Classes shows you how to effectively navigate the platform for your content classes

  • Using Your Online Textbook walks you through the steps to find and use your book online

  • Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) answers common questions that you may have

  • ELA 7 Dos & Don'ts explains what to do and what not to do for best practices

  • Enrichment & Supplemental Resources: If you find yourself up for a challenge, be sure to check out this page to practice your higher-level English skills

Meet the Teacher!

  • I am Ms. Tersigni, and you can call me Ms. T. This is my second year teaching at OHVA, but I have been teaching for a total of 13 years! I am a true English teacher at heart because I really do love reading, writing, and learning. When I am not teaching I spend my time with my family including my dog, Dallas. Going for hikes to check out Ohio parks and listening to music are passions of mine. If I am awake and not in a live class, chances are I have music playing from my bluetooth speaker, phone, or record player!
